Senior Capstone

In our Senior year of high school, the Web Design students are tasked with creating products that leaves an impact on our society that fully demonstrates all the skills and knowledge that we have learned over the course of all of our Graphic and Web Design Development. We are to do this project with one or two more classmates and only have assistance with one educational mentor and one community mentor. Using the skills that we have learned and worked on, we wil be able to create and promote a product that will leave an impact on our society which can possibly appeal to numerous clients.
